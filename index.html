About

BHUBANESWAR ENGINEERING COLLEGE (BEC) is a Co-Educational Institution specializing in Engineering and Technology under Koustuv Group of Institutions. The objective is to serve as a source of the highest learning, taking the light of knowledge to the depths of darkness, enriching the deprived by serving them with learning, which is the greatest of the world's resources.
